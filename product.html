Many households face high electricity bills without knowing the exact reason or which devices consume the most energy. VoltWise solves this by installing a compact smart meter alongside your home electricity meter — no complex setup, no rewiring.

The device connects to a mobile application that shows your real-time usage, breaks down consumption by appliance, and sends instant alerts the moment you exceed a set threshold. You finally know what is costing you money.

After analyzing your usage, VoltWise suggests practical improvements — from reducing AC runtime and upgrading to an inverter, to switching to LED lighting or installing motion sensors. Small changes, big savings.
